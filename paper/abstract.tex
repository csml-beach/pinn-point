\begin{abstract}
This manuscript skeleton accompanies the PINN-Point repository, which studies how collocation-point selection affects physics-informed neural network training on a two-dimensional Poisson problem with complex geometry. The current draft establishes a paper-ready structure around the existing codebase, experiment workflow, and artifact management conventions. Final scientific claims should be updated only after the repository's seed-aggregated experiment outputs have been curated into the paper-facing artifact directories.
\end{abstract}
